Brightwave Commerce — Customer Policy Handbook
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

---

REFUND POLICY

Brightwave Commerce offers a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all orders. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 30 days of the delivery date. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within 5 to 7 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Digital products and downloadable software licenses are non-refundable once the download link has been accessed. Gift cards and store credit are also excluded from the refund policy.

To initiate a refund, customers must contact our support team with the order number and reason for the request. Refunds for defective or damaged items will be processed without requiring the item to be returned, provided photographic evidence is submitted within 48 hours of delivery.

---

SHIPPING TIMES AND DELIVERY

Standard domestic shipping within the contiguous United States takes 5 to 7 business days from the date of dispatch. Expedited shipping (2 to 3 business days) and overnight shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

International orders ship to over 40 countries and typically arrive within 10 to 21 business days, subject to customs clearance in the destination country. Brightwave Commerce is not responsible for customs duties, import taxes, or delays caused by local customs agencies.

All orders over $75 qualify for free standard domestic shipping. Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed after 2:00 PM Eastern Time will be dispatched the following business day.

Tracking information is emailed automatically once the shipment is picked up by the carrier. If your tracking number is not active within 24 hours of receiving the dispatch confirmation, please contact support.

---

RETURN PROCEDURES

Items eligible for return must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase. Returns without original packaging may be subject to a 15% restocking fee.

To begin a return:
1. Log in to your Brightwave account and navigate to Order History.
2. Select the order containing the item you wish to return and click "Request Return."
3. Choose the return reason from the dropdown menu and submit the form.
4. A prepaid return shipping label will be emailed to you within 1 business day.
5. Package the item securely and drop it off at any authorized carrier location.

Once the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, a refund or exchange will be processed within 3 business days. Customers will receive an email confirmation when the return is complete.

Exchanges are available for items of equal or lesser value. If the replacement item is of greater value, the customer will be charged the price difference at the time the exchange is processed.
